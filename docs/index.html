Rémi Parrot

I am assistant professor within Nantes Université - École Centrale de Nantes. I belong to the STR research team at LS2N.

Research

I am mainly interested in model checking systems with time, weights and concurrency. In other words, I play with automaton, Petri nets and their extensions with time and weights.

Recently, I have been (or am currently) working on:

  • identification of Petri nets
  • expressivity of Petri nets with time
  • model checking and control on weighted timed automaton
